1. Let's Be Cops

    Two best friends, Ryan and Justin, attend their college reunion dressed up as cops. However, trouble ensues when they are presumed to be actual police officers and are made to fight gangsters.

  2. Rush Hour

    When a Chinese consul's young daughter is kidnapped, Hong Kong Detective Lee must team up with Carter, a loud-mouthed LA detective. Distinctive work styles and cultural differences pose hiccups.

  3. Night School

    After accidentally blowing up his workplace, Teddy Walker is forced to attend a night school. Soon, he is forced to deal with a group of misfit students and a teacher who thinks he is dull-witted.

  4. Great Beans of China

    Mohyi is rejected by his grandfather and uncles for being a coward. He seeks his mother's help, yet his stepfather sends him to China to participate in a cooking competition.
